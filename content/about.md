# About Bear Steps

Bear Steps is an invite-only air steps practice group based in Boston, MA.

Bear Steps is dedicated to promoting safe and responsible air step practices in the dance community.

Our mission is to provide resources, guidelines, and education to help dancers of all levels enjoy air steps while prioritizing safety for everyone involved.
