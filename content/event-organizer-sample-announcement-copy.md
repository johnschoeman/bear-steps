# Air Steps Announcement Copy for Event Organizers

<p class="subtitle"><em>Ready-to-use text for communicating air steps policies to your event participants.</em></p>

Use these sample announcements to clearly communicate air steps guidelines to your event attendees. Customize the text to match your event's specific policies and tone.

## Pre-Event Registration Communication

### Email/Website Copy

<div class="announcement-copy">

#### Air Steps Policy

We're excited to welcome air steps as part of our event! To ensure everyone's safety and enjoyment:

- **Air steps are welcome** in jam circles, competitions, and performance showcases
- **Air steps are not permitted** on the social dance floor during open dancing
- **Safety first:** Only attempt movements you've practiced and mastered
- **Consent required:** Always get clear agreement from your partner before any air step
- **Safety first:** Practice only movements you've trained for and mastered

Questions about our air steps policy? Contact us at [your email] before the event.

## Customization Tips

- **Match your tone:** Adapt the language to fit your event's style (formal, casual, fun, etc.)
- **Add specifics:** Include your venue's specific spaces, times, and contact information
- **Consider your audience:** Adjust complexity based on your participants' experience level
- **Test first:** Run announcements by experienced air steps practitioners in your community
- **Be consistent:** Use the same key messages across all communications
