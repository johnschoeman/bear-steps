Air Steps Policy

We're excited to welcome air steps as part of our event! To ensure everyone's safety and enjoyment:

  • Air steps are welcome in jam circles, competitions, and performance showcases
  • Air steps are not permitted on the social dance floor during open dancing
  • Safety first: Only attempt movements you've practiced and mastered
  • Consent required: Always get clear agreement from your partner before any air step
  • Spotting available: Trained spotters will be available during designated air steps times
